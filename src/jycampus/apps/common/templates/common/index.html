About Us

Greetings in the name of Jesus.

With great joy of being immersed in the innumerable blessings that has been showered upon us, Kerala Campus Ministry is letting you know about Kerala Campus Conference 2019 (KCC19).

Being a ministry that has played a key role in the movement in forming young missionaries, we assumed that we have a need of gathering campus leaders from all over the state to celebrate and share happiness about our missionary call to do the mission of Christ and also motivate others to do the same. This conference will be a gathering of such kind of people who are doing mission by proclaiming Jesus in various campuses so that they can learn, feel and teach different things each other which are helpful in their ministering.

Bring your 5 loaves and 2 fish and let Him multiply those into abundance in our campuses.

See you there at Bharata Mata College, Thrikkakara on August 22 - 25, 2019.

Mission 2020

MISSION 2020 is a new step initiated by campus ministry kerala, with a dream of budding God's kingdom in each campuses of kerala. And its main aim is to build a youth with Christ centered life. We expect and hope that this will happen by the year 2020.Under this project many initiatives were already started in various regions with prayers.let us together built a new generation youth with a life in christ and with more social values.

A ministry of Jesus Youth movement, serving higher education institutions in Kerala.
